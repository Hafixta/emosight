What is a Sentiment Analysis?

Sentiment analysis is an automated method to determine the feelings or opinions expressed in a text. It's one of the most fascinating subfields in Natural Language Processing (NLP), a branch of AI that studies how robots process human language. Sentiment analysis is the study of subjective information in a statement, such as opinions, assessments, feelings, or attitudes about a topic, person, or institution. Positive, negative, and neutral expressions can all be classed. Consider the following scenario:

“This website is so easy to use” → Positive
“I was able to log into my account” → Neutral
“I’m so annoyed with your website” → Negative
